ABOUT

Rachel Korosi is a Cornell University graduate who studied communication and information science with a minor in music. She has a passion for music, design, media and technology studies, and marketing and is currently working as a Music Programming Coordinator at SiriusXM. She loves to explore the field of audio engineering and supplemented her studies at Cornell with summer courses at NYU's Clive Davis Institute of Recorded Music. She also interns at Renaissance Recording, where she gets hands-on experience setting up and breaking down recording sessions. Visit the music & sound page to learn more and view a sample of her sound design work.
